What we found:

We found that an age gap exists, which Obama benefitted from. We also found that both a pro-black and anti-black bias exists and effected Obama's election.

Conclusion:

Our finding is: Although the age gap and a pro-black bias might exist, and may have played a part in Obama being elected president, they are both dwarfed by the anti-black bias that prevented Obama from experiencing the landslide victory that was predicted and expected.
