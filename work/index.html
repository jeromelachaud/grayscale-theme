Organization Description

Imagine you are state recreation department heads..
Youth level sports are a very popular activity in this country. Many club and school teams exist which require the use of referees to ensure that the game is played, played correctly, and documented.
Therefore.. a large organizational system exists in order to provide referee professionals for these games.

Problem Statement

The problems experienced by this organization are poor channels of communication between the referees and assignors and an inefficient method of assigning and re-assigning referees.

Symptoms

The symptoms of this problem are as follows: • Referees are unavailable for a certain time or day, yet they are assigned games for that time or day • Referees are unavailable for assigned days, and give late notice to the assignor who then has to find another referee to cover the game • The availability of the referees is unclear, which leads to the assignor having difficulty finding an available referee to cover a game • Referees and assignors do not communicate quickly or directly, resulting in scheduling problems • Poor communication results in referees and teams showing up to games that have been cancelled, re-scheduled, or moved to another field or location

Solution

In order to solve this problem, the following objectives must be accomplished. The system must create a better channel of communication between referees and assignors that allows for quick, if not instantaneous, responses or answers. The system must also simplify and optimize the process of assigning referees to games in order to reduce confusion and the effort that is currently made to ensure that all games are covered. The scope of this problem involves the relationship between the assignor and the referee, and extends to the management of the games, such as re-scheduling, re-assignment, and cancellation.
