The Impact of Advertising Location and User Task on the Emergence of Banner Ad Blindness: An Eye-Tracking Study

Josh Mills

2016-02-18

Auditory Icons

This paper looks at the affect of using auditory icons as a warning system in an environment where warnings are prevalent and plentiful, such as a control room or a hospital

Results

The results showed great success for the auditory icon warnings as 100% of the first group were able to correctly match the icons, the majority of the second group was able to match 100% correctly as well, and 93% of the third group was able to accurately match the icons
It turns out that this is attributed to the fact that the sounds were similar, for example both 15 and 22 were similar to a snap/slam sound. This can be solved though by simply involving the user in order to discover similar sounds.
The study was conducted in a control room environment, but I can see this being very practical for many other environments as well including airplane cockpits, cars, hospitals, etc.
